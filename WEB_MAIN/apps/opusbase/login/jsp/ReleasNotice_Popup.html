How to delete cache on Chrome:

1. Open Chrome.
2. Go to URL chrome://settings/clearBrowserData (or click Ctrl + Shift + Delete).
3. Check “the beginning of time option” and select first 4 options to delete.
4. Click Clear browsing data.
5. Close Chrome browser and restart.

How to delete cache on Internet Explorer:

1. Open Internet Explorer.
2. Go to Tools > Delete browsing history… (or click Ctrl + Shift + Delete) and select first 4 options to delete.
3. Click Delete.
4. Close Internet Explorer and restart.
